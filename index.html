Ingredients
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Optional: ice cubes, honey, or mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. Cut the lemon into wedges
  2. Place the wedges in a glass or pitcher.
  3. Pour the cold water into the glass or pitcher
  4. Stir to mix the lemon and water.
  5. Add ice cubes, honey, or mint leaves if desired.
  6. Enjoy your lemon water!
